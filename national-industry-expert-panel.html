About the Panel

Bridging classrooms and boardrooms

Higher education is at its best when students learn alongside people who have built, led and transformed real organisations. The National Industry Expert Panel is our invitation to that conversation.

The TeamLease Digivarsity National Industry Expert Panel brings together senior leaders, founders, mentors and practitioners from across India and beyond — a network committed to opening doors for the next generation of professionals.

TeamLease Digivarsity is one of India's largest collaborative higher-education ecosystems, partnering with universities and employers to make learning more relevant, applied and career-ready. This panel extends that mission, bringing industry voices directly into the student experience.

Empanelled experts engage with students through guest lectures, mentoring, career conversations, project guidance and industry interaction. The model is flexible by design: experts contribute on their own terms, on topics they care deeply about, at a pace that suits their calendars.

What unites every member of this panel is a shared belief — that experience is most valuable when it is shared, and that mentorship is one of the most enduring forms of leadership.

At a glance

  • National network of industry experts and mentors
  • Engagement through lectures, mentoring & projects
  • Flexible online or offline participation
  • One-year empanelment, renewable by mutual consent
  • Recognition across institutional platforms
