About Andhra University

Andhra University is one of India's oldest and most prestigious state universities, known for its strong academic foundation, research contributions, and commitment to student development. Established in 1926, the university has built a legacy of excellence across arts, science, commerce, engineering, and management. Through Digivarsity, Andhra University offers UGC-recognized Online programs designed to provide flexible, affordable, and accessible higher education to learners across India.

Frequently Asked Questions

Are Andhra University's Online programs UGC-recognized? +

Yes. All programs offered through Digivarsity are fully UGC-entitled.

Are these degrees valid for government jobs? +

Yes. The degrees are recognized nationwide for both government and private sector employment.

How are examinations conducted? +

Exams follow UGC-compliant formats and are conducted in designated centers or online, depending on the program.

Is online learning flexible for working professionals? +

Yes. Programs are designed for flexible learning with recorded lectures, study materials, and online student support.
